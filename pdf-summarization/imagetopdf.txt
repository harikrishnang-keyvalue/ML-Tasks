This is a Reseller Agreement between i3 Integrative Creative Solutions, LLC and Bravatek Solutions, Inc. effective from April 17, 2017.

Purpose: Company develops or provides Company Products and desires that Reseller promote and solicit commitments to buy Company Products in the Territory.
Confidentiality: Each party shall keep in trust and confidence all Proprietary Information of the other party.
Term and Termination: The Agreement shall have a term of 1 year and may be terminated by either party for non-cause with a sixty (60) written notice.
Representation and Warranty: Reseller shall make no representation, guarantee, or warranty concerning the Company Product except as expressly authorized in advance by Company in writing.
Ownership: Company grants Reseller the right to use and display the Company trademarks, tradenames, and other designations of source with prior approval.

Additional Information: The Reseller will obtain pricing quote from Company for each opportunity. The Agreement is renewable for a 1-year extension by amendment to this agreement. The Reseller shall have the exclusive right to commission for any Registered Referrals for a period of six (6) months following the termination of this Agreement. The Reseller will receive Referral Fee outlined in exhibit B even if the government elects to use a different contract vehicle. The Agreement shall be governed by and construed under the laws of the Commonwealth of Virginia without regard to the conflicts of law provisions thereof.
